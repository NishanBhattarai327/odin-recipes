Vegan Muesli

Looooooks like Vegan Muesli

A vegan version of yours mom's favorite holiday breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 and half(14 ounce) cans coconut milk, or more to taste.
  • 3 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 2 Granny Smith apples, peeled and grated
  • half lemon, juiced
  • 2 cups sliced strawberries
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup, or to taste

Steps

  1. Mix oats, coconut milk, and chia seeds together in a bowl.
  2. Toss apples and lemon juice together in a bowl until coated; fold into oat mixture.
  3. Stir strawberreis, blueberries, and maple syrup into oat mixture.
  4. Regrigerate for 8 hours to overnight.
